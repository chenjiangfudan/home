Foliation and birational geometry

The goal of this conference is to discuss recent progress in foliation theory from the perspective of birational geometry and related topics. The conference also aims to attract more graduate students to work in the field of foliation theory.

Organizers: Meng Chen (Fudan), Jingjun Han (Fudan), Chen Jiang (Fudan), Jihao Liu (Northwestern)
