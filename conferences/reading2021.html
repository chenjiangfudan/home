Reading seminar 2021 fall

We will focus on some classical papers in birational geometry. If you are interested in participating or give a talk, please contact Jingjun Han.

Organizers: Guodu Chen (Westlake), Jingjun Han (SCMS/Utah), Chen Jiang (SCMS), Wenfei Liu (Xiamen), Chuanhao Wei (Westlake)

Seminar Room: Online via Zoom, Zoom Meeting ID: 938 5377 3150

Schedule:

  • Oct. 22, 10:00 (Beijing), Lingyao Xie (Utah)

References:

  • Hacon, McKernan, Xu, On the birational automorphisms of varieties of general type. Ann. of Math. (2) 177 (2013), no. 3, 1077–1111.
  • Hacon, McKernan, Xu, ACC for log canonical thresholds. Ann. of Math. (2) 180 (2014), no. 2, 523–571.
  • Hacon, McKernan, Xu, Boundedness of moduli of varieties of general type. J. Eur. Math. Soc. (JEMS) 20 (2018), no. 4, 865–901.
  • Birkar, Zhang, Effectivity of Iitaka fibrations and pluricanonical systems of polarized pairs. Publ. Math. Inst. Hautes Études Sci. 123 (2016), 283–331.
  • Birkar, Anti-pluricanonical systems on Fano varieties. Ann. of Math. (2) 190 (2019), no. 2, 345–463.
  • Birkar, Singularities of linear systems and boundedness of Fano varieties. Ann. of Math. (2) 193 (2021), no. 2, 347–405.
